01 // ABOUT US

Our Mission & Vision

The Cyber Wings was founded with a clear purpose: to create a community of cybersecurity enthusiasts who learn, grow, and innovate together.

Education

We provide workshops, training sessions, and resources to help members develop cybersecurity skills and knowledge.

Community

We foster a collaborative environment where members can network, share ideas, and work together on cybersecurity projects.

Security

We promote ethical hacking practices and responsible disclosure to contribute to a more secure digital world.

OUR STORY

From Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2020 by a group of passionate students, The Cyber Wings has grown from a small study group into a recognized club with over 50 active members.

Our club has organized numerous workshops, participated in national cybersecurity competitions, and even hosted our own CTF (Capture The Flag) events.
